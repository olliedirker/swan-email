Explorers Wanted!

Your customers are invited join us on an adventure like no other. Whether they opt for a 10, 11-, 16- or 18-day voyage, they will be part of a select and privileged group who will get to experience true sensory overload as they get to explore some of the most exciting and remote areas on our planet.

Antarctica, South Georgia, the South Shetland, and Falkland Islands offer a combination of wildlife and scenery that is spectacular and unique. No camera can do these destinations justice, one must see and experience this personally.

With a maximum of just 152 guests each, our recently launched elegant boutique expedition ships, the SH Vega, and her sister the SH Minerva, are the perfect size. Designed to the highest standards our ships are the ultimate spot from which to enjoy the majestic scenery. Take advantage of up to 3 landings per day, truly exploring this unspoiled part of the world. In addition guests will enjoy intimate lectures hosted by our expedition and scientist team, ensuring they return home as experts and passionate explorers themselves.

Our Featured Sailing:
The Antarctica Peninsula, 11 Days.

Your clients will live out their Antarctica dream on this exceptional 11-day voyage aboard our boutique ice-class expedition ship. Their journey to this icy wilderness starts with the Drake Passage sea crossing. Get ready for fabulous photo opportunities of magnificent scenery and wildlife as we sail and explore the Antarctic Peninsula and the South Shetland Islands. This immersive cruise is the ultimate Antarctica journey that will make for the trip of a lifetime.
